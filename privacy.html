Global Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

ThinkNest Play (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides mobile applications via the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This document outlines our rigorous standards for data protection and user privacy.

1. Scope of Data Collection

We adhere to the principle of "Data Minimization." We only collect information that is strictly necessary to operate our services:

  • Service Data: IP addresses and device metadata (OS version, device model) are processed temporarily to ensure app stability and fix bugs.
  • Monetization Data: We utilize Apple and Google's native payment systems. We never see or store your credit card details.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We utilize IDFA (iOS) and AAID (Android) to deliver relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.

2. Third-Party Integrations

To provide high-performance gaming, we partner with the following entities. Each maintains its own privacy standards:

  • Google AdMob: For monetization and personalized advertising.
  • Firebase (Google): For real-time crash reporting and usage analytics.
  • Apple Game Center / Google Play Games: For leaderboards and cloud saves.

3. Push Notifications & Local Data

If you grant permission, we may send push notifications to inform you about new levels or updates. You can opt-out at any time through your device settings. All gameplay progress is typically stored locally on your device or via your encrypted Store Cloud (iCloud/Google Drive).

4. Your Legal Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Regardless of your location, we provide all users the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data associated with their device ID.
  • Request the permanent deletion of their data from our analytics and ad-partner databases.
  • Opt-out of "Personalized Tracking" directly via device privacy settings.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard encryption and security protocols. While no system is 100% secure, we continuously monitor our third-party SDKs for vulnerabilities to protect our community.

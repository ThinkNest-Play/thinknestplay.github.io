Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

ThinkNest Play ("we," "us," or "our") operates mobile games available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy details our commitment to data transparency and user privacy for our indie game studio operations.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a high-quality gaming experience and support our free-to-play model, we collect the following types of information:

  • In-App Purchase Data: We do not store financial information. All transactions are processed by Apple or Google. We receive anonymized transaction receipts to unlock digital content.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We collect mobile advertising IDs (IDFA for iOS and AAID for Android) to serve ads through Google AdMob.
  • Usage Analytics: We use Firebase to track non-personal data such as level progression, session length, and crash reports to improve performance.
  • Push Notifications: If opted-in, we collect unique push tokens to send game updates. These tokens do not identify you personally.

2. Third-Party Services

Our games utilize third-party SDKs that may collect data independently according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising and monetization.
  • Google Firebase: For analytics and stability monitoring.
  • App Store / Play Store: For processing payments and leaderboards.

3. Your Rights & Data Deletion

We respect international privacy standards, including GDPR and CCPA. While we do not use a centralized user account system, you have the right to request the erasure of data associated with your device identifier.

Requesting Deletion: Users may request the deletion of their associated data by contacting us via the email address listed below. Providing your Advertising ID assists us in locating specific records.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we identify that such data has been collected, it is purged immediately.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our games or legal requirements. We encourage users to check this page periodically.
